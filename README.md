## Instructions
- **Shoot**: Aim with mouse and shoot on left click.  
- **Jump**: Right click on mouse.  
- **Move**: A and D / left and right arrows.  
- **Dash**: S / down arrow.  
- **Exit game**: ESC  

## Story
The player ventures **beyond our solar system**, into the uncharted void, only to encounter a massive alien entity. This boss guards the frontier of space, summoning endless minions and unleashing powerful attacks. To move forward and claim victory among the stars, the player must **defeat the B.O.S.S and its army**.

## Boss
- **Appearance**: An alien overlord towering in deep space, shifting with glowing energy.  
- **Attacks**:  
  - Fires energy balls in bursts.  
  - Summons waves of flying and walking alien minions.  
  - Intensifies attacks as health drops.  

## Player Mechanics
- **Movement**: Quick, responsive dodges across the battlefield.  
- **Attack**: Fire energy projectiles with precision to weaken the boss.  
- **Collectibles**: Collect power-ups for extra powers or points.  

## Boss Phases
**Phase 1**
- Boss fires standard projectiles.  
- Spawns small groups of enemies.  

**Phase 2**
- Boss grows more aggressive.  
- Shoots faster and summons stronger waves.  
- Player must combine dodging, resource collection, and firepower to survive.  

## Level Design
- **Environment**: Outer space arena, with shifting backgrounds of stars, planets, and nebulas.  
- **Layout**: Wide open battlefield, designed for dodging waves and counterattacks.  
- **Dynamics**: Increasing intensity, with enemy spawns and escalating boss mechanics.  

## Sound
- **Effects**:  
  - Laser blasts and energy ball impacts.  
  - Alien growls and enemy spawn cues.  
  - Explosions on boss or enemy defeat.  
- **Music**: Epic space-battle soundtrack, intensifying with each phase.  

## UI
- **Indicators**: Player lives, boss health bar, time countdown, score.  

## Art
- **Assets**: Alien boss sprite, flying enemies, walking enemies, space bullets, energy balls, and glowing collectible orbs.  
- **Style**: Vibrant space palette with neon energy effects and explosive animations.
